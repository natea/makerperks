## Community source lists

Program data is also aggregated and reconciled from these community-maintained
lists (each entry records its origins in `sources[]`):

- [doanbactam/awesome-builder-programs](https://github.com/doanbactam/awesome-builder-programs)
- [Ildarflame/awesome-student-developer-deals](https://github.com/Ildarflame/awesome-student-developer-deals)
- [dakshshah96/awesome-startup-credits](https://github.com/dakshshah96/awesome-startup-credits)
- [avinash201199/awesome-startup-credits](https://github.com/avinash201199/awesome-startup-credits)
- [cloudcommunity/Free-for-Nonprofits](https://github.com/cloudcommunity/Free-for-Nonprofits)
- [tresni/awesome-nonprofit](https://github.com/tresni/awesome-nonprofit)
- [DobroslavRadosavljevic/awesome-startup-deals](https://github.com/DobroslavRadosavljevic/awesome-startup-deals)
- [athman3/awesome-free-nonprofits](https://github.com/athman3/awesome-free-nonprofits)
- The viral "$800K in AI credits" LinkedIn post (seed).

All amounts, eligibility, and links are aggregated as published by these sources
and verified-as-of the dates recorded per entry. Always confirm the current offer
on the provider's official page before applying.
